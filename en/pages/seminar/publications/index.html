Seminars

Last updated on 2026-01-08

At OUXT Polaris, we actively engage in speaking activities to share the knowledge gained through our development with the ROS community and the robot competition community. Below is a list of our past presentations.

Competitions (Technical Documentation)

In competitions like RoboBoat and the Maritime RobotX Challenge, “Technical Documentation” summarizing the technical details of vehicle development is included as a scoring item. We have published the materials written and presented by OUXT Polaris in the past.

Academic Conferences

ROSCon / ROSCon JP

This is the world’s largest international and domestic conference for ROS (Robot Operating System) developers. OUXT Polaris has had multiple presentations accepted.

ROSCon JP 2024

  • Date: 2024/09/25 (Tokyo)
  • Author: Kenta Futami, Fumiya Matsuzaki
  • Title: Maritime RobotX Challenge 2024に向けた自律航行ハードウェアとROS 2とのインテグレーション (Japanese)
  • Video: Presentation Video
  • Abstract: 自律航行船と自律ドローンを連携させたロボット競技会「Maritime RobotX Challenge」への挑戦を例に、高度なハードウェアとソフトウェアの結合手法、制約事項、ソフトウェア高速化の知見について発表。

ROSCon JP 2021

  • Date: 2021/09/16 (Tokyo/Online)
  • Author: Kotaro Yoshimoto, Masato Ogura
  • Title: Maritime RobotX Challenge 2022 のためのビヘイビアプランニング開発 (Japanese)
  • Video: Presentation Video
  • Abstract: Maritime RobotX Challenge 2022に向けた自律航行システムのビヘイビアプランニング開発について発表。コロナ禍でもチーム開発を行うためのROS 2ベースのシステムアーキテクチャ、シミュレーション環境、ビヘイビアプランニング手法を紹介。

ROSCon JP 2019

ROSCon JP 2018
